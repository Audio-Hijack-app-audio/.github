## About Audio Hijack Software

**Audio Hijack Software** provides a complete audio recording tool that captures sound from any application — Zoom, Safari, Spotify, Discord, games — with live effects processing. **Audio Hijack Software** combines app recording, audio routing, and real-time effects into one cohesive interface. **Audio Hijack Software** supports scheduled recordings, track splitting, and format conversion. **Audio Hijack Software** includes ad blocking, noise reduction, and broadcast streaming. **Audio Hijack Software** enables podcasters and video creators to record app audio without cables or loops. **Audio Hijack Software** is trusted by podcasters, YouTubers, and audio professionals.
